Features

Everything you need to
own your finances.

Privacy first

All processing happens locally on your device using a private SQLite database. No servers. No tracking. No account required.

Auto statement import

Connect Gmail once — FinAmigo automatically detects and imports bank statements from HDFC, SBI, ICICI and more, forever.

Smart categorization

Over 300 built-in rules across 35 categories — groceries, dining, UPI, EMI, subscriptions and more, tagged automatically.

FinAmigo Score™

A CIBIL-inspired financial health score (300–850) across 6 weighted dimensions. Understand exactly where to improve.

Unified dashboard

All your bank accounts and credit cards in one view. Track balances, limits, and utilization across HDFC, SBI, ICICI and beyond.

Biometric lock

Fingerprint and PIN authentication protect your data. No one can open the app without your biometric — not even us.

Salary detection

Automatically identifies your salary with periodicity analysis — even across multiple income sources and freelance payments.

AI insights (Pro)

Powered by Gemini AI — deeper categorization, natural language insights, and personalized financial summaries.
