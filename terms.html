1. What FinAmigo Is

FinAmigo is a personal finance dashboard that helps you import, organize, and visualize your bank transactions from PDFs, CSV exports, Gmail bank statements, and SMS bank alerts — all processed locally on your device.

FinAmigo is a data visualization and tracking tool only. It does not provide professional financial, investment, tax, or legal advice. Any financial decisions you make based on information shown in the App are made entirely at your own risk. FinAmigo is not a bank, lender, payment service, or licensed financial adviser.

The FinAmigo Score™ is a proprietary metric calculated locally on your device based on your imported transaction data. It is designed to give you a general sense of your financial patterns and health.
